ABOUT

White sauce pasta is pasta tossed in a creamy white sauce, typically made with butter, flour, and milk, often with added cheese for a richer flavor. This sauce is also known as béchamel sauce.

SERVES 4

INGREDIENTS CHECK LIST

  1. Penne Pasta --2.00 cup
  2. Water --6.00 cup
  3. Cheddar Cheese --0.50 cup
  4. Olive Oil-- 1.00 tsp
  5. Chicken -- 150gm
  6. Black pepper powder --0.50tsp
  7. Butter --1tsp
  8. garlic --1tsp
  9. maida --2tsp
  10. milk 2cup
  11. oregano 1/2 tsp
  12. Dried bassil 1/2 tsp
  13. salt 1/4tsp
  14. olive oil 1tsp

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil in a pot, cover and cook the pasta in the boiling water for 9 mins.
  2. Drain the pasta and add 1 tsp of Olive oil to prevent the pasta from sticking on to each other.
  3. In a pan, add 1 tsp of Olive oil, and chicken. Season this with ¼ tsp of salt and ¼ tsp of pepper. Cook the chicken for 3 mins. Remove the chicken from the pan./li>
  4. In the same pan, add 1 tsp of Olive oil, 1 tbsp of butter.
  5. Add finely chopped garlic to the pan and saute for 30 seconds.
  6. Add 2 tbsp of maida to the pan, stir until maida and butter are mixed.
  7. Add milk to the pan.
  8. Add oregano, basil, salt and pepper.
  9. By the time the sauce comes together and starts to thicken, add ½ cup of Cheddar Cheese. Add the cooked pasta and mix it with the sauce. And finally add the cooked chicken to the pan and mix well.
  10. Remove from heat and serve.
